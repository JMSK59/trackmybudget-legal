Privacy Policy

TrackMyBudget

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected by the TrackMyBudget app, why it is collected, how it is used, and which third-party services are required for the app to function.

```

1. Data collected

TrackMyBudget may collect the following data:

  • the email address used to create, secure, and manage the user account
  • the user identifier required for authentication, synchronization, and account security
  • budget data voluntarily entered by the user, including accounts, income, fixed expenses, variable expenses, debts, savings, goals, categories, household members, and transactions
  • household-related data, including added members, roles, and information required for shared budgeting
  • information related to in-app purchases, Premium subscriptions, and subscription status, managed through RevenueCat, Apple App Store, and Google Play Store

TrackMyBudget does not connect to real bank accounts, does not automatically retrieve banking data, and does not collect users’ banking credentials.

2. Use of data

The data is used to:

  • allow users to create and access their account
  • provide budgeting and personal finance management features
  • synchronize data between members of the same household
  • display dashboards, transactions, goals, and statistics
  • manage Premium features and associated access rights
  • ensure the proper functioning, security, and maintenance of the app

3. Third-party services used

TrackMyBudget uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: authentication, database, synchronization, and storage of information required for the app to function
  • RevenueCat: management of in-app purchases, subscriptions, purchase receipts, and Premium status
  • Apple App Store: processing of in-app purchases and subscriptions on iOS
  • Google Play Store: processing of in-app purchases and subscriptions on Android.

These services may process certain technical or purchase-related data only to provide the app’s features, verify subscriptions, and maintain Premium access.

4. In-app purchases and subscriptions

In-app purchases and Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store on iOS and by Google Play Store on Android. TrackMyBudget does not collect or store full payment information, such as credit card numbers.

RevenueCat is used to verify subscription status, synchronize Premium access, and allow purchase restoration when necessary.

5. Data sharing

Personal data is not sold to third parties. It is not used for advertising tracking or targeted advertising.

Some data may be processed by the providers listed above only to enable authentication, data synchronization, subscription management, and the proper functioning of the app.

6. Data retention and deletion

Data is retained as long as the user account remains active or as long as it is necessary for the app to function.

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data from within the app or by contacting support at the address provided below.

7. Security

TrackMyBudget uses secure services for authentication, data storage, and synchronization. Communications with servers are performed through secure connections.

Users remain responsible for keeping their login credentials confidential and for securing access to their device.

8. User rights

Users may request access to, modification of, or deletion of their personal data by contacting TrackMyBudget support.

9. Contact

For any question regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: trackmybudgetmobile@gmail.com

```
