Privacy Policy

TrackMyBudget

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected by the TrackMyBudget application, why it is collected, and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

TrackMyBudget may collect the following data:

  • email address used to create and manage the user account;
  • user identifier required for authentication and synchronization;
  • budget data voluntarily entered by the user, such as accounts, income, expenses, debts, savings, goals, categories, household members, and transactions;
  • information related to Premium subscriptions and in-app purchases, managed through RevenueCat and Apple.

TrackMyBudget does not connect to real bank accounts and does not automatically retrieve banking data.

2. How We Use Data

The collected data is used to:

  • provide the core budget management features of the app;
  • allow users to create and synchronize a household;
  • display dashboards, goals, transactions, and budget summaries;
  • manage access to Premium features;
  • ensure authentication and secure access to user data.

3. Third-Party Services

TrackMyBudget uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: authentication, database, and storage required for the app to function;
  • RevenueCat: management of in-app purchases, subscriptions, and Premium status;
  • Apple App Store: processing of iOS in-app purchases.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data to third parties. Data is not used for advertising tracking or targeted advertising.

Some technical data or purchase-related data may be processed by the third-party services listed above only to provide the app's features.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained as long as the user account is active or as long as it is necessary to provide the app's features. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data from within the app, when available, or by contacting support.

6. Security

TrackMyBudget uses secure services for authentication and data storage. Communications with servers are made through secure connections.

7. Children's Privacy

TrackMyBudget is not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: trackmybudgetmobile@gmail.com
