Privacy Policy

TrackMyBudget

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This privacy policy explains what data is collected by the TrackMyBudget application, why it is collected, how it is used, and which third-party services are necessary for the application to function properly.

1. Data collected

TrackMyBudget may collect the following data:

  • email address used to create, secure and manage the user account
  • user identifier required for authentication, synchronization and account security
  • budget data voluntarily entered by the user, including accounts, income, fixed expenses, variable expenses, debts, savings, goals, categories, household members and transactions
  • household-related data, including added members, roles and information required for shared budget management
  • technical data required for the proper functioning of the application, data synchronization and account security.

TrackMyBudget does not connect to real bank accounts, does not automatically retrieve banking data and does not collect the user's banking credentials.

TrackMyBudget does not collect full payment information, such as credit card numbers.

2. Use of data

The data is used to:

  • allow users to create and sign in to their account
  • provide budget management features
  • synchronize data between members of the same household
  • display dashboards, transactions, goals and statistics
  • allow the management of accounts, categories, income, expenses, debts, savings and household members
  • ensure the proper functioning, security and maintenance of the application.

3. Third-party services used

TrackMyBudget uses the following third-party service:

  • Supabase: authentication, database, synchronization and storage of information required for the application to function properly.

Supabase may process certain technical data only to provide authentication, data synchronization, account security and the proper functioning of the application.

4. Data sharing

Personal data is not sold to third parties. It is not used for advertising tracking or targeted advertising.

Some data may be processed by the service provider mentioned above only to enable authentication, data synchronization, security and the proper functioning of the application.

5. Data retention and deletion

TrackMyBudget applies a limited retention policy for budget history.

Budget history data, including transactions, goals, budget allocation goals and amount exceptions, is retained for the current calendar year and the previous calendar year.

For example, during 2026, TrackMyBudget retains budget history for 2026 and 2025. When the year changes to 2027, budget history from 2025 and earlier years may be automatically deleted.

Account data, household data, active configuration data and data required for the application to function may be retained as long as the user account remains active or as long as necessary to provide the service.

The user may request the deletion of their account and associated data from within the application or by contacting support at the address listed below.

6. Security

TrackMyBudget uses secure services for authentication, data storage and synchronization. Data exchanges with servers are performed through secure connections.

The user remains responsible for keeping their login credentials confidential and securing access to their device.

7. User rights

The user may request access to, modification of, or deletion of their personal data by contacting TrackMyBudget support.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at: trackmybudgetmobile@gmail.com
